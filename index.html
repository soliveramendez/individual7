Visit Valparaíso

A walk that connects with the history and the most beautiful views of the city. We toured its historic center, declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO. Valparaíso is a city and a community of Chile, in the central littoral of the continental territory, in the capital of the province and region of Valparaíso. It is the historic, administrative, university and main urban center of the Metropolitan Area of ​​Valparaíso, which forms together with the communities of Viña del Mar, Quilpué, Villa Alemana and Concón.
